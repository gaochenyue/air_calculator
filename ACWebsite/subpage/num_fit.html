>>数值计算>>拟合
 

1.多项式拟合

命令: polyfit([xdata],[ydata],[datalen],[n])
示例: AC:\> polyfit([-2,-1.6,-1.2,-0.8,-0.4,0,0.4,0.8],[2.8,2.96,2.54,3.44,3.56,5.4,6.0,8.4],[8],[2])

    ans=

    3.495625 1.87797619 1.061755952

2.曲面拟合


命令: curefit([xdata],[ydata],[zdata],[xlen],[ylen],[xn],[yn])
示例: AC:\> curefit([1,2,3],[5,10],[5,10;10,20;15,30],[3],[2],[3],[3])

    ans=

    15.    2.               7.5
    1.    3.996802889e-015  5.329070518e-016
    0.    0.                0.
